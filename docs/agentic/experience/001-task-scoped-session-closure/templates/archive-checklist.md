# Archive Checklist

Use this checklist after the task execution report exists.

## Before Archive

- The task objective is stated in one sentence.
- The report separates scope from non-goals.
- Changed files and generated artifacts are listed.
- Commands and checks are recorded with pass/fail summaries.
- Skipped checks include reason and residual risk.
- Follow-up work is not described as completed work.
- Reusable process lessons are linked to `docs/agentic/experience/`.
- Raw chat logs are not copied into the archive.

## Archive Target

- Completed task report: `docs/completed-tasks/YYYY-MM-DD-short-slug/README.md`
- Generalized experience: `docs/agentic/experience/NNN-short-slug/README.md`
- Candidate skill: `docs/agentic/experience/NNN-short-slug/skills/<skill>/SKILL.md`
- Agent role card: `docs/agentic/experience/NNN-short-slug/agents/<agent>.md`
- Templates or prompts: `docs/agentic/experience/NNN-short-slug/templates/`

## After Archive

- Add the completed task to `docs/completed-tasks/README.md` when applicable.
- Add the experience to `docs/agentic/experience/README.md` when promoted.
- Link reports to related task cards, evidence bundles, or experience records.
- Keep the final user-facing response short and point to the durable report.
